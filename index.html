Who We Are

Founded in 2012, Eco Brand Japan sources and curates luxury handbags and accessories from iconic houses like Hermès, Chanel, Louis Vuitton and more, giving premium pieces a new chapter. We operate within the circular economy, respecting each bag's craftsmanship and ensuring authenticity at every step.

With a team of experienced experts, we authenticate, restore and prepare items so that they can bring joy across generations. Our collection is hand-selected for quality and rarity, making it possible for you to own a piece of fashion history responsibly.

Our Promise

Authenticity

Every item we offer is thoroughly vetted by specialists. Our commitment to transparency means you receive only genuine pieces and honest descriptions.

Sustainability

We believe luxury should not be disposable. By keeping beautiful creations in circulation, we honour the materials and craftsmanship that went into them.

Respect & Trust

Our relationships with suppliers and clients are built on respect and trust. We aim to make buying and selling pre-owned luxury a pleasant, transparent experience.

How We Work

For Suppliers

  • We purchase your inventory upfront.
  • Photo catalogs showcase your products to our network-created by us or by you.
  • In-house authentication and valuation for all stock.

For Distributors

  • Daily-updated catalog with tens of thousands of items by brand and category.
  • Expert advice on curating selections for your client segments.
  • Consignment options on major marketplaces and luxury retail platforms, online and in store.
